Problem 6: Container With Most Water


Problem Statement: Find two lines that form a container holding the most water. 
•	Formula: Area = min(height[i], height[j]) * (j - i)


Greedy Insight:
•	Start with the maximum width (i=0, j=n-1). 
•	Move the pointer pointing to the shorter line. Moving the taller line can only decrease the area, but moving the shorter one might find a taller line to compensate for lost width. 

Pattern
•	Two pointers with greedy elimination


Core Thinking
•	Area = width × min height
•	Move smaller pointer
